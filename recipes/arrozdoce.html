Arroz Doce

One of the most tradicional portuguese dessert

Ingredients
  • 1 liter of milk
  • 200g of sugar
  • 200g of rice
  • coffespoon of salt
  • lemon peel
  • 3 egg yolk
  • cinnamon
Preparation
  1. Bring the milk, sugar, rice and salt to the heat in a pan and when it comes to a boil, introduce the lemon peel.
  2. Simmer on a low heat, stirring occasionally, until the rice is well cooked but still brothy.
  3. Crush the yolks with a fork in a bowl, add a little cooked rice, stir and mix everything in the pot.
  4. Return to very low heat, and stir for about a minute.
  5. Remove from heat and distribute in individual bowls or on a deep platter.
  6. Once cold, sprinkle with cinnamon.
